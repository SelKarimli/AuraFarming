About AuraFarming

Born from the need to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology, AuraFarming is revolutionizing agriculture in Azerbaijan and beyond.

Our Story

The idea for AuraFarming emerged during a bootcamp in Zangilan in May 2025, where our team had the opportunity to interact directly with farmers and villagers. Their main challenge was the difficulty in obtaining financial support. This observation led to the formation of the idea to create a unified platform that brings farmers and investors together, fostering collaboration and sustainable growth in agriculture.
